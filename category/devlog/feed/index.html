Steam Inventory Helper (SIH) continues to improve, offering Steam users new capabilities for inventory management, profile customization, and simplified trading. With updates from 2.3.0 to 2.4.1, the extension has become even faster, more convenient, and more functional. In this article, we’ll tell you about the main innovations that will make your life on Steam simpler and more enjoyable – from mass crafting badges to integration with new marketplaces.

Global Optimization: Faster and More Stable

Each SIH update brings performance improvements. Developers are tirelessly optimizing the extension to make it work faster and consume fewer resources. Bugs have been fixed, such as issues with loading item float values, mass adding games to cart, and currency conversion on item pages. Now you can enjoy smooth operation even with large inventories or active trading on the Steam market.

Steam Profile Customization: Configure as You Wish

One of the main and most noticeable innovations in recent versions is the profile customization editor, which allows you to change the appearance of both your profile and any others – from friends to random users.

What’s available:

  • Changing avatars, themes, mini-profiles, backgrounds, and animated backdrops.
  • The ability to save favorite elements to favorites.
  • Support for quick configuration exchange – just copy the link and paste it into the address bar of the window with saved presets. Everything will apply instantly.
  • Smart item search — now you can search not only by name but also by game name (for example, “Dota 2” will show all available elements from there).
  • Support for instant application – any purchased elements can be applied in 1 click without going into Steam settings.

What has improved: After the first release of customization in 2.3.0, SIH received a lot of user feedback, and the developers responded promptly:

  • Completely redesigned the customization section.
  • Improved search, filtering, and visual display of elements.
  • Updated customization information – added new backgrounds, animations, avatars, etc.
  • Simplified disabling and resetting customization in one click.

Now it’s not just a “cool feature,” but a full-fledged customization tool that works quickly, conveniently, and flexibly.

Quick Access and New SIH Menu

In version 2.4.0, the extension received a new navigation menu that allows you to quickly access all SIH functionality: be it customization, case simulation, Steam level boosting, market price comparison, or transaction history.

At first launch, users are greeted with an interactive overlay that explains how to use the new features. This makes SIH even more friendly to beginners and convenient for experienced users.

Trading: Sales Have Become Simpler and Faster

One area that is constantly improving is trading and inventory management.

New features:

  • Quick sale of items on SIH.app directly from the Steam inventory.
  • Ability to sell a group of items in a couple of clicks.
  • Fixes and improvements to market integration: fixed issues with currencies, button display (“Inspect in game”, “Instant sell”), and frequent errors when loading items.
  • New behavior when relisting items in Steam – now adapted to the latest platform changes.

Additionally, you can now use items directly from SIH – for example, a previously purchased profile background can be applied to your account immediately, bypassing lengthy visits to Steam settings and menus.

Free Items and Sales

During the Steam Spring Sale, a special button for automatically collecting free items appeared in SIH. An information window has also been added showing which items were received through SIH.

This is a great aid for those who want to grab freebies without spending time on it – SIH will do everything for you.

Under-the-Hood Improvements

Over dozens of minor versions (from 2.3.1 to 2.3.9 and beyond), developers focused on optimization, bug fixes, and increasing stability:

  • Accelerated loading of float values.
  • Fixed errors with mass adding games to cart.
  • Fixed bugs with the display of buttons and pages in the Steam interface.
  • Updated integration with DMarket.
  • Added support for the new Avan.Market marketplace in the price comparison block.

Technical Improvements and Redesign

Among other changes:

  • Mass badge crafting – if you’ve accumulated many card sets, now you can craft them all at once.
  • Improved interface on the friends page: now you can see the date when the friendship began, as well as the trading history with this user.
  • New option — disabling the visual display of transaction history if you want to maintain privacy.
  • Ability to locally reset SDA files if authorization problems occur.

CSFloat Integration

With the release of 2.4.1, SIH received integration with CSFloat, making it even more convenient for traders and skin collectors. Now float values can be viewed, checked, and analyzed directly through SIH.

Why Update?

Steam Inventory Helper is not just an extension, but a real assistant for gamers and traders. With each update, it becomes faster, more functional, and closer to your needs. Want a stylish profile? Customize it in two clicks. Need fast trading? Use new marketplaces and instant selling. Collecting badges? Craft them in bulk!

The latest version 2.4.1 is already available for download. Install the update and discover how SIH turns Steam into an even more convenient and exciting platform. Your ideas and wishes are always taken into account – join the community and share your impressions!

The developers of Steam Inventory Helper continue to actively improve the extension, adding useful features and promptly addressing issues. Updates 2.2.7–2.2.11 bring numerous improvements that make using Steam more convenient, especially for traders, collectors, and active platform users. This article provides an overview of all the updates and enhancements released in January 2025.

Enhanced Game Statistics on Steam Store Pages

Starting from version 2.2.7, users can view expanded statistics on Steam game pages. The following information is now available:

  • Total number of reviews, including hidden ones: This helps provide a more objective assessment of a game’s popularity. For example, this is crucial if reviews are region-specific or there is a trend of removing comments.
  • Last update date and a link to its description: This is particularly useful for multiplayer games, allowing you to quickly determine whether a project is actively supported.
  • Peak player counts over the past 24 hours and all-time peaks: This feature is invaluable for those who prefer online games with an active player base.
  • Price history and lowest recorded price of a game: You no longer have to rely solely on Steam sales—you can easily track price changes and buy games under the most favorable conditions.

Practical Benefits: With these improvements, users can quickly assess whether a game is worth its price, whether it is actively updated, and how popular it currently is.

Notifications About Free Items

Another new feature is automatic notifications about free items. The extension checks for freebies every 24 hours and sends a notification directly to your browser. This is especially relevant for those collecting items like in-game skins, cards, or exclusive time-limited items.

Use Case Example: You can set up notifications to receive timely information about giveaways, helping you expand your collection without additional costs.

New Tools for Managing Inventory

  • Version 2.2.9 fixed a bug with the Instant Sell button in the inventory. (If this button doesn’t work for you, ensure it’s enabled in the settings under Settings > Inventory > “Instant Sell” Button ON/OFF.) This feature allows you to instantly list items for sale at the current market price. It’s particularly useful for quickly selling items without manually listing them.
  • Version 2.2.10 introduced additional inventory management tools. Items can now be sorted by the value of stickers in Counter-Strike 2. This allows users to easily identify items with charms and determine their market value before selling weapons or the charms themselves.

Additional Information on Item Pages

One of the most useful additions in version 2.2.10 is the ability to see which case an item comes from (available after clicking the “Show more info” button). This feature is extremely beneficial for collectors and traders who buy or actively open cases.

Why This Matters: Knowing an item’s origin allows for a more accurate evaluation of its value, particularly for rare or limited collections. For example, some items are produced in limited quantities, and information about the case or collection can influence purchasing decisions. This is also relevant when selecting items as long-term investments or those that may soon be removed from the in-game store.

Additionally, a new feature tracks active buy orders while promoting items to the top. This helps avoid errors when listing offers and enables more profitable deals.

Currency Conversion on SIH.Market

The introduction of built-in currency conversion has become one of the most sought-after features. Users can now convert prices between different currencies directly on the website.

Practical Value: This feature is especially useful for international traders working with various regions. For instance, you can immediately understand the cost of an item in your local currency, even if the deal is made in a different region.

Interaction History

Version 2.2.11 added another important feature—interaction history with users. On the page of any friend or trading partner, you can now see:

  • How long ago you added this user.
  • The number of trades sent and accepted.
  • The date of your last interaction.

Usage Scenarios: This feature is invaluable for players who frequently trade. For example, you can quickly assess how long someone has been in your friends list and check their activity to avoid interacting with inactive or suspicious accounts.

Other Important Improvements

The developers also introduced several minor yet significant changes:

  • The ability to relist multiple items from your inventory at once. This saves significant time, especially for users actively managing a large number of items.
  • Fixed issues with weapon pattern displays, making it easier to search for rare items.
  • Resolved bugs in the trade window, ensuring proper display of game subsections.

Steam Inventory Helper strives to make the extension as convenient as possible for all user categories. From enhanced game statistics to new tools for traders, there’s something useful for everyone.

Stay updated with SIH to keep track of the latest features and be among the first to take advantage of them!

Our extension has recently been updated to version 2.2.2. In this article, we’ll walk you through the main new features and changes. Some of these were highly requested by users—we listened and delivered. Others required adjustments due to VALVE’s updates, ensuring smooth and stable operation.

Steam Update: What Happened?

Every Tuesday night into Wednesday, Steam undergoes routine maintenance, typically addressing bugs and stabilizing the client. Unexpectedly, the December 3rd update introduced changes closely tied to our extension’s features.

On any skin’s page in the Steam launcher, users can now see details about applied stickers, charms, and patches on agents. This functionality has been part of our extension for years, and it seems VALVE has finally decided to borrow the idea. We don’t mind, but it did cause issues where our data overlapped with the “Inspect in Game” button, shifting it to the right.

Aside from basic display functionality, VALVE didn’t add anything new. However, we had to quickly resolve these interface conflicts. Now, everything is working smoothly, and you can continue using SIH to access detailed information on item pages, your inventory, trade history, and other sections.

Remember, beyond showing applied items, SIH provides details like float values, pattern indices, sticker wear levels, and more. While we’re flattered that VALVE acknowledges our work, we remain committed to enhancing our extension with new features and tools.

Charm Pattern Display

We actively process feedback from our users. Since charms were introduced, one of the top requests was to add information about their patterns.

Why is this important?
Some charms have unique designs directly linked to their pattern values. Users have grown accustomed to SIH’s convenient functionality for skins and dislike endlessly launching the game to check item details.

We immediately began work on this feature. Now, you can easily check a charm’s pattern on its page, in your inventory, or even when applied to a weapon. Hunting for rare charms just got simpler—head to the marketplace and grab the best deals faster with Steam Inventory Helper.

Detailed Item Information

Providing the most comprehensive details about each skin, sticker, charm, and more is one of SIH’s standout features. This function is invaluable for both investors and regular users. However, such detailed data often takes up too much space.

Now, you can toggle the text descriptions as needed, allowing you to hide or expand information with a click.

Wallet Balance History Graph

In today’s world, tracking your finances is essential. With our new feature, you can view the history of your wallet balance. These graphs help you analyze the growth or decline of your inventory’s value over time and identify the reasons behind the changes.

Perhaps you made a profitable investment, experienced losses during VALVE’s large events, or encountered other circumstances.

To access your graph, open the extension, locate your balance under your nickname in the top-right corner of your browser, and dive into your account’s balance history.

Steam Trading Cards

Steam trading cards are periodically awarded during gameplay, completing tasks, or randomly. These cards are full-fledged items that can be traded or sold on the marketplace. By using them, you can craft badges and earn rewards like profile emojis or backgrounds.

Unused trading cards often pile up in inventories, waiting to be used or sold. With SIH, you can quickly check how many unused cards you have, decide whether to sell them, or put them to good use.

Steam Inventory Helper continues to evolve, offering users tools that make navigating and optimizing their Steam experience seamless. If you haven’t explored these new features yet, now’s the perfect time!

Steam Inventory Helper (SIH) is a powerful extension for Steam users, designed to automate various tasks related to inventory management, trading, and profile customization. Recent updates have significantly expanded its functionality, making it even more user-friendly and efficient. In this article, we’ll delve into all the new changes.

Inventory Updates

  1. Custom Currency Exchange Rates
    SIH now allows users to set custom currency exchange rates. This feature is especially useful for traders:
  • When trading with users from other countries, adjusting the rate provides more accurate price displays.
  • You can align the exchange rate with your region to simplify planning purchases and sales.
  1. Item Highlighting System
    A new item highlighting system has been introduced, categorizing inventory items by their rarity:
  • Items are highlighted in distinct colors based on quality (e.g., Mil-Spec, Restricted, Covert, etc.).
  • The settings menu offers customization options, such as fully coloring the inventory slots or just the borders. A dedicated slider is available under the “Inventory” section.
  1. Price Inclusion
    Previously, item prices did not account for attached stickers. Now, SIH automatically includes sticker values in the overall item price. This update is vital because:
  • Stickers can significantly increase an item’s value.
  • Collectors and traders can now see precise data, simplifying inventory valuation.
  1. Fixes for the Delete Button
    The delete button for selected items has been fixed to address previous errors. The updated functionality includes:
  • Removing profile comments with a single click, saving time.
  • Resetting all selected items in the inventory without bugs.
  • Clearing the Steam wishlist entirely with one click.

These improvements make inventory interactions smoother and more reliable.

Support for External Marketplaces and the Steam Store

  1. New Marketplace Integrations
    SIH now supports five additional platforms:
  • DMarket
  • CSMoney
  • Csmarket.gg
  • IGXE
  • Tradeit

Users can now view item prices across all supported marketplaces simultaneously. This streamlines finding the best deals, eliminating the need to manually switch between sites. Note: This functionality is limited to buying or selling items and does not apply to trading skins for marketplace items.

  1. Steam Points Shop Integration
    For users focused on inventory aesthetics, a unique feature has been added:
  • SIH automatically scans the Steam Points Shop and collects all available free items, such as profile backgrounds, emoticons, and animations.
  • With one click, you can claim all available items, ensuring you don’t miss any free rewards from Steam.

This feature is perfect for those who love personalizing their profiles without extra effort.

Settings Updates

  1. Redesigned Interface
    The settings menu has been redesigned for a more modern and user-friendly appearance. This makes it easier for newcomers to navigate and customize their SIH experience.
  2. Settings Export
    A new export feature allows users to save and transfer their SIH settings:
  • Save your preferred configurations, including filters, highlighting options, and interface settings.
  • Upload these settings to another device for seamless restoration.
    For example, if you use Steam on multiple devices, you can configure SIH once and export the settings for easy transfer.
  1. Overhauled FAQ Section
    The Frequently Asked Questions section has been updated to reflect the new features, making it easier to find answers related to setup, trading, and other aspects of SIH.
  2. Enhanced SDA Functionality
    The Steam Desktop Authenticator (SDA) now includes:
  • A quick logout option for Steam accounts.
  • One-click deletion of cloud data, improving security management.

Steam Inventory Helper continues to evolve to meet user expectations. These updates make SIH one of the most convenient tools for managing inventories and trading on Steam. If you haven’t updated SIH yet, now is the perfect time to do so and explore the new features.

Start enjoying the benefits today!

Counter-Strike 2: A Classic Reborn with Modern Innovations

Counter-Strike 2, or CS2, is the latest chapter in the Counter-Strike legacy, a game that has defined tactical shooters for over two decades. Valve’s new installment retains the familiar, adrenaline-pumping gameplay fans know while introducing impressive visual and mechanical upgrades. In CS2, the core premise remains: two teams, Terrorists and Counter-Terrorists, engage in objective-based battles that test aim, reflexes, and teamwork. But this time, it’s reimagined with the latest in game technology to elevate the experience.

Stunning Visuals with Source 2

CS2’s visual transformation is powered by the Source 2 engine, which brings iconic maps like Mirage, Dust2, and Overpass to life in stunning detail. Textures are sharper, lighting is realistic, and environments feel more immersive, creating a more engaging backdrop for gameplay. Smoke grenades, now rendered with dynamic volumetrics, interact with surroundings like never before, responding to gunfire and changing the flow of a match. These upgrades make CS2 both a tactical and visual feast, enhancing the immersion in every round.

Gameplay Enhancements for Precision and Skill

CS2’s refined weapon mechanics offer players more control, with reworked recoil and spray patterns that reward practice and precision. A revamped matchmaking system has been introduced, making it easier for players to find fair and balanced matches that reflect their skill level. Valve has also integrated a robust anti-cheat system, ensuring that every match is secure and competitive—a significant improvement that builds player confidence in the game’s integrity.

Community-Driven Content and Professional Esports

CS2 has reinvigorated both the casual and competitive scenes, with players contributing custom maps, mods, and skins that extend the game’s life and variety. The professional esports scene has quickly adopted CS2, showcasing its upgraded mechanics on the world stage, with top players and teams embracing the new possibilities. This community involvement enriches CS2, creating a thriving ecosystem of players, creators, and fans.

Final Thoughts

Counter-Strike 2 combines the best elements of the Counter-Strike series with modern innovation, creating an experience that feels fresh while honoring its roots. With advanced visuals, precise mechanics, and a supportive community, CS2 is set to define a new era for tactical shooters. It’s a must-play for anyone seeking intense, skill-driven gameplay, proving that Counter-Strike’s legacy is stronger than ever.

Counter-Strike 2, often abbreviated as CS2, is the latest iteration in the legendary Counter-Strike franchise, developed by Valve Corporation. Building on the legacy of its predecessor, Counter-Strike: Global Offensive (CS), CS2 introduces a new era of tactical, team-based first-person shooter (FPS) gameplay with a modernized look and feel, refined mechanics, and immersive new features. The game’s core principles remain the same: two teams—Terrorists and Counter-Terrorists—battle across various objective-based maps, competing in intense rounds that test skill, strategy, and teamwork.

Graphics and Engine Overhaul

One of the standout features in CS2 is its graphics and engine overhaul. Moving from the Source engine to the latest Source 2 engine, CS2 delivers more realistic visuals and smoother gameplay. This update includes enhanced lighting effects, improved textures, and more lifelike physics, giving maps like Dust2, Inferno, and Mirage a fresh, immersive feel. Smoke grenades, for instance, now behave more dynamically, filling spaces naturally and dispersing under gunfire, adding an extra layer of tactical complexity.

New Features and Improved Gameplay

CS2 also brings an array of new gameplay features designed to elevate the classic Counter-Strike experience. Subtle but impactful changes include reworked weapon recoil and spray patterns, giving players even greater control and precision in firefights. The matchmaking system has been upgraded to ensure fairer and more balanced matches, with skill-based ratings that reward progression and improvement. A new anti-cheat system has also been implemented, further strengthening CS2’s competitive integrity and creating a more enjoyable experience for all players.

Community and Competitive Scene

CS2’s dedicated fanbase has seamlessly transitioned from CS, maintaining its position as one of the most popular and enduring FPS games worldwide.

Counter-Strike 2 is a testament to the franchise’s enduring appeal and Valve’s commitment to evolving a beloved game while preserving its core essence. Whether you’re a seasoned player or a newcomer, CS2 offers an engaging, high-stakes experience that reflects the very best of what competitive gaming has to offer.
